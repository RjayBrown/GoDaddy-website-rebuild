About Us

Who We Are

Intuitive Data Driven Decisions Corp. (ID3) is an information sourcing organization created to support growth and stability in the communities we serve. Our mission is to increase accessibility to resources for education and career development. We believe that knowledge and opportunity will improve engagement, empowering students, professionals, and business owners to successfully navigate the rapidly changing economic landscape.

What We Do

Our primary focus is on the creation of individualized learning paths through the REACH Empowerment Initiative, helping community members grow and thrive. We research industry trends and simplify access to available resources, ensuring that every learner has the freedom and ability to develop the knowledge and skills needed to achieve their goals.

A Comprehensive Approach

We provide information by curating quality local college programs, certifications, and industry-specific training, helping members explore opportunities in:

  • STEM
  • Healthcare
  • Manufacturing
  • Business, and more!
